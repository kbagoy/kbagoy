Who is Kate?

After earning a BFA in graphic design, I started my career working for Nike. While I loved the design culture, I loved tech more and left the berm to earn an MBA in Marketing & move to Silicon Valley to work as a product manager at a mobile startup. While the bay area wasn't a fit, I've been working with startups and new businesses since -- I love helping companies grow, and I can't imagine only doing one type of design job every day!

I am also a photographer, an animal lover, a volunteer, a constant learner, a world-traveller, an organizer, an artist, an avid reader and, at the core, a problem solver – I thrive when I can observe, analyze, organize and take projects apart only to put them together in a new and creative way.
