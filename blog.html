About Me

After earning a BFA in graphic design, I started my career working for Nike. While I loved the design culture, I loved tech more and left the berm to pursue an MBA in Marketing & move to Silicon Valley to work as a product manager at a mobile startup. While the bay area wasn't a fit, I've been working with startups and new businesses since.
